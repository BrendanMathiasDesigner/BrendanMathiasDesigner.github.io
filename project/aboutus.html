About Us

Estabblished in 1887 by Master pastry chef Eric De Vallance Madelaine Patisserie has consistently set the standards by baking cakes and pasteries of the finest textures and favourites.

Madelaine Patisserie is a firm favourite among Londoners, francophiles and french expats. Despite its speciality of cakes, breads and pasteries Madelaine Patisserie is famed for its selection of gourmet coffees.

Madelaine Patisserie is consistently voted in the top three Patisseries in London with a wide variety of french breads, cakes, pasteries, chocolates, viennoseries, tarts all made to exceptionally high standards.

Patrons simply adore the superb food, equisite cakes, pasteries, fine coffees and excellent service given to all Madelaine Patisserie customers.
