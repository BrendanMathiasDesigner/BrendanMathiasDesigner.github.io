Our Breads

We pride ourselves in the quality of our breads the baking of bread to the highest possibe standards was one of the core priciples on which Chef Eric De allance founded Madelaine Patisserie.Bread is freshly baked on the premises every day by our specialist boulangers using the finest ingredients and according to French recipes personally refined by Chef Eric De Vallance and handed down the various generations of the De Vallance family. Including French favourites such as baguettes, pains au chocolat and croissants come visit us and experience French baking at its finest.
