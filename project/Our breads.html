Our Breads

We pride ourselves in the quality of our breads the baking
of bread to the highest possibe standards was one of the core priciples
on which Chef Eric De Vallance founded Madelaine Patisserie.Bread is freshly

Including French favourites such as baguettes, pains au chocolat and
croissants come visit us and experience French baking at its finest.
